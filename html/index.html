About Me

My passion for technology began at a young age, experimenting with software, coding, and automation. Over the years, I have developed a keen interest in artificial intelligence and software engineering.

Beyond coding, I am fascinated by science and research. I constantly seek to expand my knowledge by reading scientific papers, engaging in discussions, and applying insights to my projects. This website is a reflection of my experiences and interests. Thank you for visiting!
